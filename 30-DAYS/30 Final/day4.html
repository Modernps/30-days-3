Importance of MFA

Multi-Factor Authentication is essential because it:

  • Enhances Security: Adds an extra layer of protection beyond just a password.
  • Reduces Risk: Lowers the chances of unauthorized access.
  • Increases User Confidence: Builds trust with users.
