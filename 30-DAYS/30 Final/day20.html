Account Privacy Challenge

Securing your Google Privacy settings

Your Google account holds a vast amount of personal information, from search history and emails to location data and payment details. Without proper privacy settings, you risk exposing sensitive data to advertisers, third-party apps, or even cybercriminals.

Risks of Poor Google Privacy settings

  • Excessive Data Collection:Google tracks your online behavior for targeted ads and recommendations. Without control, this can lead to intrusive ads and oversharing of your personal preferences.
  • Security Breaches: A weakly secured account is vulnerable to hacking, phishing, and identity theft, putting your data and finances at risk.
  • Location Tracking: Google can store your movements, which can be exploited by malicious actors if not properly controlled.
  • Data Misuse: Hackers can use your personal details to impersonate you or commit fraud.

Challenge Steps

Task:Use Google Privacy Checkup

Steps to Follow:

  • Sign into your Google Account
  • Go to Privacy Checkup: Click "Data & Privacy" and Scroll down to the section "Privacy Checkup" and click on "Get Started".
  • Review Your Activity Controls: You'll be guided through the steps for Web & App Activity, Location History and YouTube History
  • Manage Your Ad Settings: You'll be prompted to adjust your ad preferences, you can choose to turn them on or off.
  • Review Sharing and Data Settings: Look at who has access to your peersonal information across Google services. You can control your profile information and External Apps connected to your Google Account.
  • Check Security Recommendations: Google provides you the option to even update your weak passwords or review suspicious account activity.

Task: Turn off location Sharing

Steps to Follow:

  • Sign into your Google Account
  • Access Data & Privacy Settings: Click "Data & Privacy"
  • Find Location History: Scroll down to the "History Settings" section and click on "Location History"
  • Turn Off Location History: There will be a switch next to "Location History" click on it to turn it on & off and click the "Pause" when prompted.
  • Manage Location Sharing with Other People: You can review your location sharing with other also in Location Sharing. If you are click on "Manage Location Sharing" and turn off sharing with others.

Task:Adjust Ad personalisation

Steps to Follow:

  • Sign into your Google Account.
  • In the left-hand menu, click “Data & Privacy.”
  • Scroll down to the “Ad Settings” section and click on “Ad Personalization".
  • Toggle “Ad Personalization” on or off.
  • Customize ad preferences by removing specific interests or categories.
  • Review and confirm your changes.

Task: Enable Multi-Factor Authentication

Steps to Follow:

  • Sign into your Google Account.
  • Click on “Security” in the left-hand menu.
  • Scroll down to “2-Step Verification” under “Signing in to Google.”
  • Click “Get Started.”
  • Verify your identity by entering your password.
  • Select your second verification method (e.g., Google prompts, SMS, or Authenticator app).
  • Follow the prompts to complete the setup.
  • Click “Turn On” to enable 2-Step Verification.

Task: Set up security alerts

Steps to Follow:

  • Sign into your Google Account.
  • Click on “Security” in the left-hand menu.
  • Scroll down to the “Security Alerts” section.
  • Click “Manage security alerts.”
  • Choose your alert preferences (email or phone notifications).
  • Click “Save” to confirm your changes.
