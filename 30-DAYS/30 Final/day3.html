Bossing your Passwords

Complete Guide to Using Password Managers on Windows & Mac OS

Password managers act like secure vaults for your login info, so you only need to remember one master password. They generate strong passwords and autofill them for easy access. Whether you use Windows or Mac, setting one up is quick and keeps your accounts safe!

What is a Password Manager?

A password manager is a tool that securely stores and organizes your passwords. It allows you to remember only one master password, while it generates and stores the rest of your passwords safely.
